## Rebuttals (memorize)
- "Lovable builds this in minutes?" → "Correct — a website takes minutes now, which is why we didn't build a website builder. Karyo is the loop after the artifact: persistent business context, research, executable actions, and week-over-week adaptation. Lovable forgets you when the tab closes."
- "Isn't this a ChatGPT wrapper?" → Show state: profile schema, feedback table, plan diff. Wrappers are stateless; this is conditioned on stored outcomes.
- "Why multi-agent, not one prompt?" → Separation lets us cache research per locality and reuse across businesses; also independent fallback per stage on free tiers.
- "How do you afford inference?" → Routing by task complexity across Gemini/Groq/OpenRouter free tiers, aggressive caching; unit economics designed for ₹0.
- "GBP/WhatsApp integration real?" → Copy-ready content + deep links today; official APIs on roadmap. We don't scrape at runtime — compliance stated upfront.
- "What breaks at 100 users?" → Free-tier RPD ceilings; answer: per-locality research cache + paid DeepSeek/Gemini tier at ~$1-2/mo equivalent unit cost, funded by Growth tier.
